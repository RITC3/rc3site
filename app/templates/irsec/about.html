About

What is IRSeC?

The RIT Competitive Cybersecurity Club's (RC3) annual end of the year competition. It stands for Incident Response Security Competition and is focussed towards students who are interesed in cybersecurity.

How can I get involved?

You can sign up to be a blue team member on the signup page. If you would like to white team for this event and help us run the competition you can sign up by emailing the executive board on the contact us page
